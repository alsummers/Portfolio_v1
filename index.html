About

Before entering into the world of programming, I had a history with illustration, 3D modeling and design, and printing. When I discovered that programming could be a creative tool I dove right into the many components of the development world. I started taking online lessons and even learning from a few programmers I had encountered while working on independent video games, before taking an in depth course on develpment at Eleven Fifty Academy.

My history in working with a team, often remotely, allows me to work easily in a collaborative environment. And I do enjoy seeing all the inventive ways others in the team can solve problems! The team environment is definiately were I thrive.

Utilizing my prior skills, I have used programming as a creative tool and would love to bring that perspective to any project. Outside of programming, I enjoy hiking (whenever I can), camping, writing and illustrating, and visiting art museums to dive into the wonderful combinitation of art, culture and history.
